Revenue Model Formulas
Run Clubs: Weekly Turnout × 10% × $28 × 52 weeks
Running Stores: Locations × (engagement ÷ 10 × 30 units) × $14 × restocks/yr  [CLT hub = 6 restocks, others = 4]
Running Influencers: Reach (buyer pool) × 0.2% × $28 × 2 posts/yr  [reach = 10% of audience if not set, capped at 5k]
Ultra Community: Community size × 8% × $28 (high engagement)
Community Runners: Reach × 5% × $28 (word of mouth)
Run Events: Turnout × 5% × $28 per event
★ Charlotte Hub leads receive a +25% local boost on all estimates.
